About this website
Design Intentions

The design of this website was chosen with our situation in mind. A dark mode was implemented as a convenience as well as a nod to the power outage based game that the robot will compete in. The website also is a mobile-first design. We focused on phones and mobile devices because most people at competition will have their phone with them and will likely view the site that way. This doesn't mean that the desktop site was neglected, just that we focused on our target audience.

Credit

The website programming and design was done by Jake Williams. Various members of the team were consulted for opinions on design. The Icebreaker's logo on the main page of the website was done by Gentry Needham. The image credit goes to all-free-download.com and we are using the image with proper credit to them.

Development

The site was built using HTML5, CSS3, and Javascript. Bulma CSS framework was used for its versatility and more effecient devlopment speed. The site and its source is hosted on github.
