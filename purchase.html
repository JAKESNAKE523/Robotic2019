About the product

Kelvin is designed to help restore power in emergency situations. The robot can repair electric lines and restore power to entire neighborhoods in an effecient manner. For the first edition of our robot, the supplies are limited. The robot uses a simplistic design to be more versatile. Businesses can easily tweak Kelvin to their workflow.



The program source is located on github here so you can understand it and tweak controls for your driver. This can help the robots controls adapt to people of all kinds, even ones with disablities.
